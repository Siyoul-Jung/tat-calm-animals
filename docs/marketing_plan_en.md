# TAT for Animals — Marketing Plan

**Scope:** Pre-launch preparation + 3 months post-launch  
*Last updated: April 2026*

---

## 1. Target Audience

**Core target:**
Animal owners who sense their pet is carrying something — unresolved anxiety, trauma, or behavioral changes that a vet visit hasn't solved. They're looking for a gentle, natural approach.

| Segment | Profile | Approach |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Core** | Existing TAT / TATLife subscribers | Direct conversion — trust already established |
| **Warm** | Holistic pet care & animal healing interest | Educate through content, then convert |
| **New** | Pet owners actively searching for solutions | YouTube search + SEO |

## 3. Channel Strategy

### 🥇 Priority 1 — Existing Subscriber Emails
The warmest audience available. These people already trust Tapas — conversion rates will far exceed any social channel.

- Early access announcement to existing TATLife subscribers
- Pre-launch waitlist (offer a free intro video in exchange for sign-up)
- Monthly newsletter: healing stories + upcoming webinar announcements
- Automated emails: webinar invitations, recording notifications, renewal reminders

### 🥈 Priority 2 — YouTube (TATLife Channel)
The channel already exists with 6,430 subscribers and 308 videos. Last upload: 5–6 months ago. Reactivation is the priority — not starting from scratch.

**Why YouTube matters most:**
- Search intent: people actively searching "dog anxiety natural remedy", "cat trauma healing"
- Evergreen content — one video drives traffic for years
- Strongest platform for the 40–60+ demographic
- Long-form video builds deep trust

**Content direction:**
- Target 2–4 uploads per month (consistency is everything)
- Animal-specific cases: "How to help an anxious dog", "TAT for cats with trauma"
- Real session footage — the moment an animal visibly relaxes is the best content
- Tapas's homepage intro video (3–4 min) → publish on YouTube too

### 🥉 Priority 3 — Podcast Guest Appearances
Zero ad spend. Reaches a warm, interested audience through trusted voices.

- Target: pet care, holistic healing, animal psychology podcasts
- Tapas shares her story + introduces TAT for Animals
- Each episode = exposure to hundreds or thousands of aligned listeners

### Priority 4 — Instagram
Repurpose YouTube footage into 30–60 second Reels. Minimal extra production effort.

- Reels: TAT session clips, animals visibly relaxing
- Stories: Tapas's day + subscriber testimonials
- Target 3 posts per week

### Priority 5 — Facebook
Organic reach is limited. Use for community engagement and paid ads once organic results are validated.

- Focus on **group participation** over page posts
- Share Tapas's content in pet owner communities
- Paid ads: only after organic channels show traction

## 4. Launch Strategy

### Phase 1 — Plant Seeds (pre-launch)
- Reactivate YouTube — resume animal content uploads *(can start now, independent of site)*
- Activate "Coming Soon" page + waitlist collection
- Begin Instagram teaser content

### Phase 2 — Build Momentum (pre-launch)
- Email existing TATLife subscribers with early access announcement
- Send waitlist an early access offer or first-month benefit
- Publish 2–3 real subscriber testimonials on social
- Tapas hosts a free live Q&A session

### Phase 3 — Launch
- Launch day: email blast + social posts simultaneously
- First 2 weeks: daily Stories updates
- First live webinar (drives The Calm Circle sign-ups)
